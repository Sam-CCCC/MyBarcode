Privacy Policy

Transparent data practices for BarcodePro users

Last Updated: March 2026

Thank you for choosing BarcodePro. Your privacy is our top priority. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle information when you use our application.

1. Information Collection

BarcodePro is designed as a client-side tool.

  • Personal Data: We do not collect, store, or share any personally identifiable information (PII).
  • Local Storage: Your configuration settings (such as font size, scaling ratios, and language preferences) are stored locally on your device via LocalStorage. This data never leaves your device.
  • Data Processing: When you import Excel files, all processing occurs within your browser's memory. No data is uploaded to any external server.

2. Permissions and Usage

To provide a full range of features, the app may request the following permissions:

  • Storage Access: Necessary for saving exported JSON configuration files or downloading generated barcode images.
  • Camera Access: Used exclusively if you utilize scanning features (if available). Images are processed locally and never transmitted.

3. Third-Party Hosting

This application is hosted on GitHub Pages. GitHub may collect certain standard log information (such as IP addresses) for security and analytics purposes. This collection is governed by GitHub’s Privacy Policy. We do not integrate any third-party advertisement trackers or analytics SDKs.

4. Data Security

Since your data is stored locally, the security of your information depends on the security of your device. We recommend not storing sensitive barcode configurations on shared or public devices.

5. Children's Privacy

Our application does not knowingly collect any information from children under the age of 13. We are committed to complying with COPPA and similar regulations globally.

6. Changes to This Policy

We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page.
